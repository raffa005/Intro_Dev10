Operating 50 years strong, Nick's Crab Shack is a relaxed and welcoming place to enjoy a variety of crab, shrimp, lobster, and any other succulent delicacy of the sea. Ever since we openned back in 1969, our motto has been fresh from the sea, untampered by man. We always aim to make sure you are getting the freshest food possible, so we make sure to leave each as close to how nature intended it to be. Come on in on Thursdays to enjoy our family crab bucket, or Tuesdays for out 2 for 1 lobster date special! Here at Nick's Crab Shack, there's always a seat for you.
